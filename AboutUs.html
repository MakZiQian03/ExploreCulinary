Our History

The recipe website "Explore Culinary" was founded in 2020 by a group of passionate food enthusiasts. What started as a simple journal to document their cooking recipes quickly gained popularity among food lovers. The unique approach in supplying Malaysian recipes resonated with readers, and within a year, the website had attracted many readers. The early years of Explore Culinary were marked by rapid growth, with continuous posting of new recipes, cooking tips, and food photography, all while engaging with growing community of followers through comments and social media.

As the webpage gained attraction, "Explore Culinary" expanded their content to include not just Malaysian traditional food recipes but also Global food recipes. The success of the blog led to numerous awards and features in major publications, cementing its place in the food website sphere.

Over the years, Explore Culinary continued to evolve by bringing on a team of contributors to keep up with the growing demand for content. The blog's success allowed it to branch out into new mediums, including a popular Facebook and Instagram pages. Today, Explore Culinary is not just a blog but a full-fledged brand, with millions of readers and followers across various platforms. It remains a beloved resource for anyone passionate about food, offering everything from simple weeknight recipes to in-depth explorations of both global and Malaysian cuisines.
