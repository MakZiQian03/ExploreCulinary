Paella Recipe

How to Cook Paella

Ingredients

  • 400g Chicken Breast
  • 250g Shrimp
  • 300g Mussels
  • 250g Squid Rings
  • 400g White Rice
  • 1 Onion
  • ½ Green Bell Pepper
  • ½ Red Chili Pepper
  • 100g Green Beans
  • 200g Peas
  • 3 Tomatoes
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • 1 Liter Fish Stock
  • 150ml White Wine
  • 50ml Spanish Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • ½ Tbsp Food Coloring (optional)
  • 1 Lemon (optional)
  • Saffron
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. Dice the chicken breast. Heat half of the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat, then add the chicken and cook until slightly golden. Remove the chicken to avoid overcooking and let the remaining oil continue to heat in the pan.
  2. Add the shrimp and squid rings to the pan and sauté for a few minutes.
  3. Add the remaining olive oil to the pan and heat. Add the chopped onion, green bell pepper, and red chili pepper. Then, add the minced garlic and diced tomatoes (or crushed tomatoes for a simpler option). Season to taste and add a few strands of saffron.
  4. Meanwhile, heat the fish stock in a separate container. The stock should be hot when added to the rice to ensure good texture.
  5. Continue cooking the ingredients in the pan for about 10 minutes until the tomatoes lose their moisture. Then, add the rice and stir frequently to prevent it from sticking to the bottom.
  6. After 5 minutes, reduce the heat to medium and add the chicken. Begin adding the fish stock gradually while stirring. If using food coloring, add it along with the stock.
  7. At this point, stop stirring the rice and let it cook for about 20 minutes. After 10 minutes, add the peas and green beans. Around 18 minutes, start tasting the rice to check for doneness.
  8. While the rice is cooking, heat the white wine in a large pot. Once it starts boiling, add the mussels and cook for about 8 minutes until the shells open. Discard any unopened shells and drain the liquid.
  9. Once the rice is done, remove the pan from the heat and arrange the mussels on top. Cover the pan with a cloth and let it rest for about 10 minutes before serving.
