Neapolitan Pizza Recipe

How to Make Neapolitan Pizza

Ingredients

  • 600 ml Water - 2.5 cups, Room temperature
  • 1 kg Le 5 Stagioni Napoletana Pizza Flour (35 oz. Tipo 00)
  • 30 grams Salt - 5 teaspoons
  • 1-2 grams Fresh yeast - Half teaspoon

Instructions

  1. To make authentic Neapolitan pizza, pour 600ml of water into a mixing bowl and add 30g salt.
  2. Mix well using your hand to dissolve the salt.
  3. Add 100g (10% of flour) to the water and mix it through with your hands until the flour dissolves, resulting in a crepe/pancake consistency.
  4. Add the yeast to the mixture and use your fingers to help it dissolve. Mix through with your hand again.
  5. Add the 5 Stagioni Neapolitan Flour, a small amount at a time, mixing with one hand as you go.
  6. Once the dough starts to come together, flip it onto a bench and start kneading it with both hands, absorbing the remaining flour.
  7. Keep working the dough until it has a smooth consistency.
  8. To check if the dough is ready, press down on it with one finger. If it bounces back, it's ready. Alternatively, use a thermometer to check that the dough's core temperature is between 23°C – 26°C (73.4°F – 78.8°F).
  9. Let the dough rest on a flat surface, covered with a damp cloth for at least 2 hours.
  10. After 2 hours, cut the dough into 250g (8.8 oz) pieces and form them into smooth, round balls.
  11. Place the dough balls in an airtight container and let them rest at an ambient temperature (16°C – 18°C / 60.8°F – 64.4°F) for 24 hours.
  12. After 24 hours, sprinkle flour onto the bench, place the dough on top, and press down using your fingers to form a small round base with a "cornicione" (crust).
  13. Gently stretch the dough by picking it up and slightly stretching it onto one forearm, then flipping it onto the bench. Repeat before shaping it into a circle.
  14. To make a Neapolitan Margherita, spread Italian tomato sauce onto the base using the back of a spoon in a circular motion.
  15. Add fresh basil leaves, a sprinkle of dry oregano, a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil, and thinly sliced cherry tomatoes.
  16. Place the pizza in a woodfire oven at 400°C (757°F) to cook, or use the max temperature on your home oven with a pizza stone.
