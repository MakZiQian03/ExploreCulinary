Tom Yum Seafood Soup Recipe

How to Cook Tom Yum Seafood Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 litre Prawn Stock
  • 2 Lemongrass, bruised
  • 5-6 Makrut Lime Leaves
  • 1 inch Galangal, sliced thinly
  • 1-2 Coriander Roots (optional)
  • 2-3 Thai Bird’s Eye Chillies, lightly mashed
  • 2-3 tbsps Nam Prik Pao (Tom Yum Paste)
  • 200g Prawns
  • 100g or 5 Fishballs
  • 1 small Squid
  • 1 loose cup Mushrooms
  • 1 tbsp Fish Sauce, or to taste
  • 1 tsp Sugar, or to taste
  • 2-3 tbsps Lime Juice, or to taste
  • 2-3 stalks of Coriander

Instructions

  1. Pour prawn stock into a pot and add the bruised lemongrass, galangal, makrut lime leaves, bird’s eye chillies, and coriander roots. Watch the video for ingredient preparation. Bring the stock to a boil, then lower the heat to a gentle simmer. Let this simmer for about 15 minutes or as long as possible to infuse the flavors.
  2. Once infused, remove all aromatics from the stock.
  3. Stir in the nam prik pao (tom yum paste) until fully incorporated with the stock.
  4. Add the seafood of your choice: prawns, fishballs, and squid.
  5. Add mushrooms and season with fish sauce and sugar. Stir to combine and allow the seafood and mushrooms to cook thoroughly.
  6. Once cooked, immediately turn off the heat. Add lime juice with the heat off. Stir to combine and taste before serving. Adjust with more lime juice, fish sauce, or sugar if needed.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot!
