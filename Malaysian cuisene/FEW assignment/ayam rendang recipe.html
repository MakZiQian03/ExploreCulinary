Ayam Rendang Recipe

Ingredients:

- 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces

- 4 tablespoons of vegetable oil

- 2 stalks of lemongrass, smashed

- 6 kaffir lime leaves

- 4 bay leaves

- 2 cinnamon sticks

- 400ml of coconut milk

- 200ml of water

- 100g of toasted desiccated coconut (kerisik)

- Salt and sugar to taste

Spice Paste:

- 10 dried chilies, soaked in hot water

- 5 shallots

- 4 cloves of garlic

- 1 inch of ginger

- 1 inch of galangal

- 1 teaspoon of turmeric powder

- 1 teaspoon of ground coriander

Instructions:

1. Blend all the spice paste ingredients into a smooth paste.

2. Heat the oil in a large pot and fry the spice paste over medium heat until fragrant and the oil separates.

3. Add the lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, bay leaves, and cinnamon sticks, and fry for another 2 minutes.

4. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are lightly browned.

5. Pour in the coconut milk and water, and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 1-1.5 hours, stirring occasionally.

6. Add the toasted desiccated coconut (kerisik), and cook until the rendang is thick and the chicken is tender. Adjust the seasoning with salt and sugar.

7. Serve hot with steamed rice or lemang.
