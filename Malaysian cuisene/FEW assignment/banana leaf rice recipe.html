Banana Leaf Rice Recipe

Ingredients:

- 2 cups steamed white rice

- 1 cup dhal curry

- 1/2 cup mixed vegetable stir-fry

- 1/2 cup potato curry

- 1/2 cup fried crispy papadums

- 1 banana leaf

Instructions:

1. Lay the banana leaf on a plate or flat surface.

2. Scoop the steamed rice onto the center of the banana leaf.

3. Arrange the dhal curry, mixed vegetables, and potato curry around the rice.

4. Serve with fried papadums on the side.

5. Enjoy the meal by mixing the rice with curries and vegetables, using your hands.
