Biryani Recipe

Ingredients:

- 2 cups basmati rice

- 500g chicken or lamb, cut into pieces

- 1 onion, sliced

- 2 tomatoes, chopped

- 1/2 cup yogurt

- 2 tablespoons biryani masala

- 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste

- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder

- Salt to taste

- 1/4 cup oil or ghee

- Fresh coriander and mint leaves for garnish

Instructions:

1. Rinse and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes. Cook until 70% done and set aside.

2. Heat oil or ghee in a pot and saut√© the onions until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes.

3. Add the chicken or lamb, turmeric, and biryani masala. Cook until the meat is browned.

4. Stir in the yogurt, tomatoes, and salt. Cook until the oil separates from the gravy.

5. Layer the partially cooked rice over the meat. Top with fried onions, coriander, and mint leaves.

6. Cover and cook on low heat (dum) for 20 minutes until the rice is fully cooked.

7. Serve hot with raita and salad.
