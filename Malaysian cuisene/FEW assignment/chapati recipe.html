Chapati Recipe

Ingredients:

- 2 cups whole wheat flour (atta)

- 1/2 teaspoon salt

- 1 tablespoon oil

- 3/4 cup water (adjust as needed)

Instructions:

1. In a bowl, mix the whole wheat flour, salt, and oil.

2. Gradually add water and knead the dough until it becomes soft and pliable. Let it rest for 20 minutes.

3. Divide the dough into small balls.

4. Roll each ball into a thin, round chapati using a rolling pin.

5. Heat a griddle or tawa, and cook the chapati on both sides until golden brown spots appear.

6. Serve hot with curry or dhal.
