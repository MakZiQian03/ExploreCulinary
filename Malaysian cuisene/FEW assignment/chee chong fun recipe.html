Chee Chong Fun Recipe

Ingredients:

- 1 cup rice flour

- 1/4 cup tapioca flour

- 2 cups water

- 1/2 teaspoon salt

- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

- 2-3 stalks of scallions, finely chopped

- 1/4 cup soy sauce

- 2 tablespoons oyster sauce

- 1 tablespoon sugar

- 1 teaspoon sesame oil

- 1 tablespoon fried shallots (optional)

Instructions:

1. In a large bowl, combine rice flour, tapioca flour, water, and salt. Mix until smooth.

2. Lightly oil a heatproof dish or tray and pour in a thin layer of the batter.

3. Steam the batter over boiling water for about 10-12 minutes, or until it sets.

4. Repeat the process until all the batter is used up, ensuring each layer is steamed thoroughly.

5. Once all layers are steamed, let them cool slightly, then cut them into strips.

6. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the chopped scallions and cook until fragrant.

7. Add the cut Chee Chong Fun strips to the pan and stir-fry for a few minutes.

8. Mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, sugar, and sesame oil in a small bowl. Pour the sauce over the Chee Chong Fun and stir well to coat.

9. Garnish with fried shallots if desired and serve hot.
