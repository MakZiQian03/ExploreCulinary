Herbal Eggs Recipe

Ingredients:

- 6 large eggs

- 4 cups water

- 1 tablespoon soy sauce

- 1 tablespoon dark soy sauce

- 2 tablespoons Chinese tea leaves (or 2 tea bags of black tea)

- 2-3 slices of dried tangerine peel (optional)

- 1 tablespoon Chinese five-spice powder

- 1 tablespoon salt

- 1-2 pieces star anise

- 1-2 pieces cinnamon stick

- 1 tablespoon sugar (optional)

Instructions:

1. Place the eggs in a pot and cover with cold water. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat.

2. Once boiling, reduce the heat to a simmer and cook for 8-10 minutes.

3. Remove the eggs and transfer them to a bowl of ice water to cool.

4. In a separate pot, combine water, soy sauce, dark soy sauce, tea leaves, tangerine peel (if using), Chinese five-spice powder, salt, star anise, and cinnamon stick. Add sugar if desired.

5. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 10 minutes.

6. Gently tap each egg on a hard surface to crack the shell all over.

7. Add the cracked eggs to the simmering herbal mixture and ensure they are fully submerged. Simmer for at least 30 minutes.

8. Remove the eggs from the mixture and peel. Enjoy warm or cold.
