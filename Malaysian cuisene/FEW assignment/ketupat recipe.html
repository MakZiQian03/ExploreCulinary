Ketupat Recipe

Ingredients:

- 2 cups of uncooked rice (traditionally long-grain)

- 6-8 woven palm leaves or ketupat casings

- Water for boiling

Instructions:

1. Rinse the rice thoroughly under cold water to remove excess starch.

2. Fill each woven palm leaf casing with about 2/3 full of rice. Ensure the rice has enough space to expand during cooking.

3. Prepare a large pot of boiling water, ensuring there is enough water to fully submerge the ketupat.

4. Place the filled ketupat in the boiling water, ensuring they are fully submerged.

5. Boil for about 3-4 hours. You may need to add more water to the pot as it evaporates.

6. Once cooked, remove the ketupat from the water and allow them to cool.

7. Slice open the ketupat casing and serve the rice dumpling with satay, rendang, or any of your favorite dishes.
