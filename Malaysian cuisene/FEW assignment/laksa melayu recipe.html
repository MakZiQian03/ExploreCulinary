Laksa Melayu Recipe

Ingredients:

- 500g laksa noodles (thick rice noodles)

- 300g mackerel or sardine, cooked and flaked

- 1 liter of fish stock

- 2 tablespoons tamarind paste

- 2-3 fresh chilies, blended

- 1 onion, chopped

- 1 lemongrass stalk, bruised

- 1 cucumber, julienned

- 1 bunch of mint leaves

- 1/2 pineapple, thinly sliced

- Salt and sugar to taste

Instructions:

1. In a large pot, heat the fish stock and add the tamarind paste, blended chilies, chopped onion, and bruised lemongrass.

2. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for about 15 minutes. Add salt and sugar to taste.

3. Add the flaked fish to the broth and simmer for another 5-10 minutes.

4. In a separate pot, cook the laksa noodles according to package instructions, then drain.

5. Serve the noodles in bowls, ladle the hot broth over, and top with cucumber, mint leaves, and pineapple slices.
