Traditional Baked Moon Cake Recipe

Ingredients:

- 400g of flour

- 250g of golden syrup

- 1/2 tsp of alkaline water

- 100g of vegetable oil

- 600g of lotus seed paste

- 4 salted egg yolks

- Egg wash (1 egg yolk mixed with 1 tbsp of water)

Instructions:

1. Mix the golden syrup, alkaline water, and vegetable oil in a bowl.

2. Gradually add flour to the mixture until a dough forms. Knead until smooth.

3. Cover the dough with plastic wrap and let it rest for at least an hour.

4. Divide the dough into equal portions and flatten each portion.

5. Place a salted egg yolk inside each portion of lotus seed paste, then wrap the dough around the paste.

6. Press the dough into a mooncake mold, then release it onto a baking tray.

7. Brush the mooncakes with egg wash and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 15 minutes.

8. Let the mooncakes cool before serving.
