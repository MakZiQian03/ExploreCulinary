Murukku Recipe

Ingredients:

- 2 cups rice flour

- 1 cup urad dal flour

- 1 teaspoon cumin seeds

- 1 teaspoon sesame seeds

- 1/2 teaspoon asafoetida

- Salt to taste

- 1 tablespoon butter

- Water as needed

- Oil for deep frying

Instructions:

1. In a bowl, mix the rice flour, urad dal flour, cumin seeds, sesame seeds, asafoetida, salt, and butter.

2. Add water gradually to form a smooth dough.

3. Fill the dough into a murukku press and shape it into spirals.

4. Heat oil in a pan and deep fry the murukku until golden brown and crispy.

5. Drain on paper towels and let it cool completely before storing.
