Nasi Kandar Recipe

Ingredients:

- 2 cups steamed basmati rice

- 1 cup chicken curry

- 1/2 cup beef rendang

- 1/2 cup okra (lady's finger) stir-fry

- 1 boiled egg, halved

- 1/4 cup pickled vegetables

- Papadums for serving

Instructions:

1. Serve the steamed basmati rice on a plate.

2. Arrange the chicken curry, beef rendang, and okra stir-fry around the rice.

3. Add the boiled egg halves and pickled vegetables on the side.

4. Serve with papadums for extra crunch.
