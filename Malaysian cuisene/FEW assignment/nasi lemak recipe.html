Nasi Lemak Recipe

Ingredients:

- 2 cups of rice (preferably long-grain)

- 1 cup coconut milk

- 2 cups water

- 1 pandan leaf, knotted

- 1 small piece of ginger, sliced

- 1 teaspoon salt

- 1 cup dried anchovies, fried until crispy

- 1/2 cup roasted peanuts

- 2 boiled eggs, halved

- Sliced cucumber

- Sambal (spicy chili paste)

Instructions:

1. Rinse the rice and drain it well.

2. In a rice cooker or pot, add the rice, coconut milk, water, pandan leaf, ginger slices, and salt.

3. Cook the rice until it's fully done. The coconut milk will give it a rich and fragrant flavor.

4. Serve the rice on a plate with crispy fried anchovies, roasted peanuts, boiled eggs, cucumber slices, and sambal on the side.

5. Enjoy your nasi lemak with any additional sides, like chicken rendang or fried chicken.
