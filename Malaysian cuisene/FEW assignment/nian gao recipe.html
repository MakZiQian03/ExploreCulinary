Nian Gao Recipe

Ingredients:

- 300g glutinous rice flour

- 200ml water

- 150g brown sugar or white sugar

- 1/4 tsp salt

- 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil

Instructions:

1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the glutinous rice flour, sugar, and salt.

2. Gradually add water to the mixture while stirring until smooth.

3. Grease a steaming tray or cake pan with vegetable oil.

4. Pour the mixture into the prepared tray or pan.

5. Steam over high heat for about 30-40 minutes, or until the mixture is set and firm. You can check doneness by inserting a toothpick; it should come out clean.

6. Let the Nian Gao cool completely before removing from the pan.

7. Slice and serve as desired. Nian Gao can be enjoyed steamed, stir-fried, or in a sweet soup.
