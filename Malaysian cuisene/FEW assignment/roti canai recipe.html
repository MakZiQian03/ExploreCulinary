Roti Canai Recipe

Ingredients:

- 2 cups all-purpose flour

- 1/2 cup water

- 1/4 cup ghee or melted butter

- 1/4 teaspoon salt

- 1/4 cup condensed milk

- Oil for frying

Instructions:

1. In a bowl, mix the flour, water, ghee, salt, and condensed milk to form a soft dough.

2. Knead the dough until smooth and let it rest for 2 hours.

3. Divide the dough into small balls and roll them out thinly.

4. Heat oil on a griddle or pan and cook the roti canai until golden brown and crispy on both sides.

5. Serve hot with dhal or curry.
