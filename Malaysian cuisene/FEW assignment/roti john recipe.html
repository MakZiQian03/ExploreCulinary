Roti John Recipe

Ingredients:

- 4 baguettes or long sandwich rolls

- 300g minced meat (chicken, beef, or mutton)

- 2 eggs

- 1 onion, finely chopped

- 2 cloves garlic, minced

- 1 tablespoon curry powder

- Salt and pepper to taste

- Butter or margarine for frying

- Mayonnaise, chili sauce, and ketchup for serving

Instructions:

1. Heat some butter or margarine in a pan and saut√© the chopped onions and minced garlic until fragrant.

2. Add the minced meat and cook until browned. Stir in the curry powder, salt, and pepper.

3. In a bowl, beat the eggs and pour them into the meat mixture. Stir until the eggs are cooked and well combined.

4. Slice the baguettes in half lengthwise and spread the meat and egg mixture evenly on each side.

5. Fry the filled baguettes in a pan until crispy and golden brown.

6. Serve hot with mayonnaise, chili sauce, and ketchup.
