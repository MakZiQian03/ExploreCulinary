Satay Recipe

Ingredients:

- 500g chicken, beef, or lamb, cut into bite-sized pieces

- 1 onion, finely chopped

- 2 cloves garlic, minced

- 2 tablespoons soy sauce

- 1 tablespoon turmeric powder

- 1 tablespoon coriander powder

- 1 tablespoon cumin powder

- 1 tablespoon sugar

- Salt and pepper to taste

- Skewers for grilling

- Peanut sauce for serving

Instructions:

1. In a bowl, mix the chopped onion, minced garlic, soy sauce, turmeric powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, sugar, salt, and pepper to create a marinade.

2. Add the meat to the marinade and mix well. Let it marinate for at least 2 hours, preferably overnight in the fridge.

3. Preheat your grill or barbecue.

4. Thread the marinated meat onto skewers.

5. Grill the skewers over medium heat, turning occasionally, until the meat is cooked through and slightly charred.

6. Serve the satay hot with peanut sauce on the side.
