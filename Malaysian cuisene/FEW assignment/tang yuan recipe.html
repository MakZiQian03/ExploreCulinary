Tang Yuan Recipe

Ingredients:

- 200g glutinous rice flour

- 100ml water (adjust as needed)

- 100g sesame paste or red bean paste (for filling)

- 50g sugar

- 1 tablespoon of sesame seeds (optional)

- 1 tablespoon of chopped peanuts (optional)

Instructions:

1. In a mixing bowl, combine glutinous rice flour and water. Mix until a smooth dough forms. Add more water if the dough is too dry.

2. Divide the dough into small balls, about the size of a marble.

3. Flatten each ball slightly and place a small spoonful of sesame paste or red bean paste in the center. Pinch the edges to seal and roll into a smooth ball again.

4. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Gently drop the filled dough balls into the boiling water.

5. Cook the Tang Yuan until they float to the surface, then let them cook for another 2-3 minutes. Remove with a slotted spoon.

6. In a separate pot, bring some water to a boil and dissolve sugar to make a sweet syrup.

7. Serve the Tang Yuan in bowls with the sweet syrup. Optionally, garnish with sesame seeds or chopped peanuts.
